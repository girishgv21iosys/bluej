HR Foresight

A new way to manage legal questions faced by HR professionals.

How HR Foresight Works

HR Foresight is platform that quickly and accurately predicts how your employment matter would be decided if it went before a court or tribunal.

Input your scenario

Fill out a brief questionnaire with the facts of your unique employee situation.

HR Foresight Analyzes

Platform uses artificial intelligence to compare your situation to all employment situations in that area of law.

Review Prediction

Review how the courts would likely decide your case and read a tailored explanation of the result, and a list of commonly referenced cases.

Document and share

Download a PDF of HR Foresight's prediction of your unique scenario and share with key stakeholders.

Get accurate and actionable answers.

HR Foresight is tested by leading employment law firms in Canada and is 90% accurate on average. Predictions are based on updated and complete case law.

Make cost-effective and timely decisions.

The software generates predictions within minutes so you can avoid costly external counsel and time-consuming research.

Quantify your risks.

The software predicts the likely outcome if your case goes to court, giving you the power to understand your position and determine next steps with confidence.
